Metaobjects

Lagoon is a run-time polymorphic type-erasure for the compile-time metaobjects from the Mirror library.

While the reflexpr operator and the related library of metaobject operation templates are proposed for standardisation, the Lagoon library is not.

Metaobjects

The Lagoon library wraps the metadata obtained from the metaobject types from the Mirror library, into instances of the shared_metaobject and shared_metaobject_sequence classes.
